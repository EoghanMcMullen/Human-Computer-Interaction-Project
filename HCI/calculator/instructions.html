You are presented with a table. The table is divided into three columns, subject, level and grade.

On the second line of the table you will see a drop down menu below the heading "subject".

Step 1: Click on the drop down menu and choose one of the subjects you are taking.
Step 2: Now choose the level of that subject by clicking on the drop down menu to the right of the subject you have just chosen. You can choose higher,ordinary or foundation.
Step 3: Now to the right you can choose the grade of that subject from A1 - NG(no grade) from a drop down box.

Move to the next line and repeat steps one to three until you have entered all of the subjects you wish to check.

On the second last line of the table you will see an option for LCVP. You can choose the grade you achieved/wish to achieve following instructions in step 3.

When you have input all the information you want you are ready to calculate your points. On the last line of the table you will see a button "Calculate Points"
Press this button. Your points will appear in the box to the right of the button.
